Google Pixelbook

the high performance Chromebook.

From 999€

Features

One, two, three, four ways to use it.

Use it as a laptop, or watch your favorite shows. Fold it into a tablet, or stand it up. Pixelbook’s unibody is 10.3 mm thin, and ready to go with an all-day battery.

The first laptop with the Google Assistant built in.

Use your voice, press the Assistant key, or circle with Google Pixelbook Pen2 to get help from Google Assistant.

Tethers instantly.

If you find yourself without Wi-Fi, Instant Tethering lets you use your Pixel phone's data connection, so you can keep going. Just like that.

Technical Specifications

Display 12.3" 2400x1600 (235 ppi) Quad HD LCD display for sharper images
400 nits for using in bright areas
Touchscreen display with Google Pixelbook Pen support
72% NTSC color
Processor Powered by a 7th Gen Intel® Core™ i5 or i7 processor for faster browsing and gaming, and seamless 4K output to an external monitor.
Battery 41 Whr battery
Fast charging: up to 2 Hrs in 15 min., or 7.5 Hrs in 60 min
45W charger (5V/3A, 9V/3A, 15V/3A, 20V/2.25A)
USB-C™ 45W adaptor that also works with Pixel phones
Memory & Storage 8GB or 16GB RAM for seamless multitasking
128GB, 256GB, or 512GB (which also supports NVME) Solid State Drive
I/O Ports Two USB-Cs for charging, 4K display output and quicker data transfer
3.5 mm headphone jack
